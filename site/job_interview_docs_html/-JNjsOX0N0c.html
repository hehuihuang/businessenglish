02 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

TK: Can you tell me about a time when you suffered a setback and had to maintain your enthusiasm? The interviewer may ask questions like this to understand how you perform in stressful situations. They may ask about your past experience, or a hypothetical situation. Similar questions include: Tell me about a time you dealt with a difficult client or coworker. Tell me about a time you responded to a crisis. Tell me about a time you gave difficult feedback. How would you handle a situation where you and your supervisor disagreed about an issue or course of action?

ZH

TK：你能讲一次你遭遇挫折、但仍然保持积极态度的经历吗？面试官问这种问题，是想了解你在压力情境下如何表现。他们可能会问你真实发生过的经历，也可能给你一个假设场景来考察你的应对方式。类似的问题还包括：讲一次你处理难缠客户或同事的经历。讲一次你应对危机的经历。讲一次你不得不给出负面反馈的经历。如果你和上司在某个问题或行动方案上意见不一致，你会怎么处理？

03 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

TK: Fear of public speaking is very common. I'm in the same boat. Where do you see yourself in five years? Common question: "Where do you see yourself in five years?" This is a chance to share your goals and show you understand what's realistic. It also shows your ambition. Consider variations: where do you see yourself in one year, and where do you see yourself in ten years?

ZH

TK：害怕公开演讲很常见，我也一样。那你觉得五年后你会在哪里？常见问题：“五年后你希望自己处于什么位置？”这是一个让你表达职业目标的机会，同时也要体现你对“在一定时间内能实现什么”有现实判断。它也会反映你的野心与动力。你还可以顺带思考两个变体：一年后你会怎样？十年后你会怎样？

04 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

Start with: "In five years, I would like to be ___." Practice out loud. If you only practice in your head, it will feel much more stressful when you're in the room speaking for the first time. Record yourself and critique: what was unclear, and how can you say it more concisely?

ZH

可以用这句话开头：“五年后，我希望自己能成为/处于__。”一定要大声练习。如果你只在脑子里想，到了面试现场第一次开口会更紧张。把回答录下来并自我复盘：哪里不清晰？怎样能更简洁地表达？

05 Question Drill

EN

RS: Yes. Imagine you're looking back on this hiring decision in a year. The person you hired has exceeded your expectations. What did he or she do that impressed you most?

ZH

RS：有的。假设一年后你回顾这次招聘决定：你录用的人远远超出了你的预期。那么他/她做了什么，最让你印象深刻？

06 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

TK: Great question. In one year, the person would have spent time learning from the team and long-time employees, then started making informed changes. I want the team to be a well-oiled machine: bouncing ideas off each other, coming up with new designs, and making headway into new markets. RS: That's helpful. It's good to know the expectations. Can you tell me a little more about the team I'd be working with?

ZH

TK：好问题。我认为一年内，这个人会先花时间向团队和资深成员学习，然后基于充分理解再做出有依据的调整。我希望团队像一台运转良好的机器：彼此交流想法、产出新的设计，并在新市场上取得实质进展。RS：这很有帮助，知道你们的期待很重要。你能再介绍一下我将要共事的团队吗？
