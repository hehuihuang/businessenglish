Top 10 Behavioral Software Engineering Interview Questions

How To Use It

01 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

1. Tell me about yourself: Keep it short, simple, and memorable. Focus on a few points you want the interviewer to remember.

ZH

1. 请自我介绍：要短、要清楚、要好记。抓住 2 到 3 个你希望对方记住的重点来讲。

02 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

2. Tell me about a project you worked on: Explain the challenges, successes, and impact of your work on the team.

ZH

2. 讲一个你做过的项目：说明项目难点、成果，以及你对团队/业务产生的影响。

03 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

3. Disagreement questions: Describe a time you disagreed with a manager or cross-functional partner, and show how you listened, collaborated, compromised, and reached a good outcome.

ZH

3. 分歧/冲突类问题：讲你与经理或跨团队伙伴发生分歧的经历，重点展示你如何倾听、协作、妥协，并最终达成双赢的结果。

04 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

4. Technical fluency: Tell a story about explaining a technical concept to a non-technical person. Translate jargon into plain language.

ZH

4. 技术表达能力：讲一次你把技术概念解释给非技术同事的经历，把术语转成对方听得懂的“人话”。

05 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

5. Handling constraints: Share how you handled deadlines or tradeoffs (for example, refactor vs. ship). Explain your options, reasoning, and the impact.

ZH

5. 约束与取舍：说明你如何应对紧迫截止期或权衡取舍（例如“重构 vs 先上线”），讲清楚你的备选方案、选择理由和最终影响。

06 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

6. Weakness you’re working on: Be honest and self-aware, and show that you’re coachable and actively improving.

ZH

6. 你正在改善的弱点：坦诚、具备自我认知，并体现你愿意被指导、在持续改进。

07 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

7. Career goals: Be upfront about how you want to grow (industry, projects, front-end vs. back-end, manager vs. IC) and align it with the company’s direction.

ZH

7. 职业目标：直说你想如何成长（行业方向、项目类型、前端/后端、走管理还是专家路线），并尽量与公司的发展方向对齐。

08 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

8. Why do you want to work for this company? Do your research, understand the company’s goals, and connect them to your interests and strengths.

ZH

8. 为什么想来这家公司？提前做功课，理解公司的目标与方向，并说明它如何与你的兴趣和优势匹配。

09 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

9. Why do you want to leave your last company? Stay respectful, explain what didn’t work, and focus on why the new role is a better fit. If laid off, be honest and calm about the situation.

ZH

9. 为什么离开上一家公司？不要贬低前公司，客观说明不匹配之处，并强调新机会更适合你。如果是裁员，也要坦诚、冷静说明情况。

10 Question Drill

Use this as a practice prompt and answer it aloud before checking the translation.

EN

10. What are you looking for in your next role? Share what you’re excited about (mentorship, learning, technical growth) and what kind of team relationship you want.

ZH

10. 你在下一份工作中期待什么？说明你最看重的点（导师机制、学习机会、技术成长等），也可以表达你希望的团队协作与管理方式。
