<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Virtual interviews still require the same professionalism as in-person interviews, plus extra preparation for your tech setup and environment.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">线上面试依然需要和线下面试一样的专业度，只是你还要额外做好设备与环境的准备。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 1: Download, set up, and test the interview platform at least 24 hours in advance. Charge your device, and log in about 10 minutes early so you can settle in.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 1：至少提前 24 小时下载、注册并测试面试平台。面试前给设备充满电，并提前约 10 分钟登录，留出调整状态的时间。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 2: Choose a location with strong internet (Wi-Fi) and good lighting. Keep the light in front of you, not behind you, so the interviewer can see your face clearly.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 2：选择网络稳定、信号强的地点，并确保光线充足。灯光或窗光要在你前方，避免背光，让面试官能清楚看到你的脸。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 3: Test your microphone, speakers, and audio settings before the interview starts. Make sure your headset and sound devices work properly.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 3：提前测试麦克风、扬声器/耳机以及音频设置，确保设备能正常收音和播放。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 4: Remove distractions. Tell people around you not to interrupt, and make sure the room is quiet with minimal background noise.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 4：清除干扰源。提前告知家人/同事不要打扰，确保房间安静、背景噪音尽量少。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 5: Dress professionally, just as you would for a face-to-face interview. Your appearance signals your standards and seriousness about the role.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 5：着装要正式，就像参加线下面试一样。外在形象会传递你的职业标准和对岗位的重视程度。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 6: Look into the webcam, not the screen. Practice answering questions while keeping eye contact with the camera.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 6：看摄像头，不要一直盯着屏幕。提前练习对着摄像头回答问题，效果更像“眼神交流”。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 7: Prepare your opening answer to “Introduce yourself.” Use the SEAT structure: Skills, Experience/Education, Achievements, and Type of person.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 7：提前准备“请自我介绍”的开场回答。可以用 SEAT 结构：Skills（技能/优势）、Experience/Education（经历/教育）、Achievements（成就）、Type of person（你是什么样的人/工作风格）。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 8: Use confident body language: sit upright, smile naturally, listen actively, and stay positive. Recording yourself the day before can quickly improve how you come across on camera.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 8：用自信的肢体语言：坐姿挺直、自然微笑、认真倾听、保持积极。面试前一天录一段自我练习视频，能很快改善镜头表现。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 9: Prepare strong answers to common interview questions (for example: “Why do you want to work here?”, “What are your strengths and weaknesses?”, “Why should we hire you?”, and “What do you know about our company?”).</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 9：提前准备常见面试题的高质量答案（例如：“为什么想来我们公司？”“你的优缺点是什么？”“为什么要录用你？”“你对我们公司了解多少？”等）。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Tip 10: Bring three questions to ask at the end. For example: “How has this role evolved over the years?”, “What is the company culture like?”, and “What are the business plans for the next few years?”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">建议 10：面试最后准备好 3 个反问问题。例如：“这个岗位这几年有哪些变化？”“公司的文化氛围如何？”“未来几年公司有什么业务规划？”</div>
