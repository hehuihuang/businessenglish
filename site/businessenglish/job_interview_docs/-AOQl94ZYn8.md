<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>One of the first questions you might hear is, “How would you describe yourself?” or “Tell us a little bit about yourself.” It’s an open question, so you can talk about your personality and interests—for example: “I’m very outgoing and I love working with others,” “I’ve always loved technology, and I’m excited to work more closely with it,” or “I’ve always loved teaching people new things.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">面试一开始常见的问题是：“你会如何形容自己？”或“简单介绍一下你自己。”这是一个很开放的问题，你可以从性格和兴趣切入，比如：“我性格外向，喜欢与人合作。”“我一直热爱科技，很期待能在工作中更深入地接触它。”“我一直喜欢教别人新东西。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Another common question is about why you want the job, such as: “Why do you want to work for us?” or “Why are you applying for this job?” A strong answer shows you know something about the company—for example, you can say you admire their work in the community or for customers, or mention something they did recently that impressed you.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">另一个常见问题是你为什么想要这份工作，比如：“你为什么想来我们公司？”或“你为什么申请这个岗位？”好的回答要体现你了解这家公司，例如你欣赏他们在社区或客户服务方面的工作，或者提到他们最近做的一件让你印象深刻的事情。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You can also connect your strengths to what the company does—for example: “I’ve always loved working with people, and I know your company specializes in helping people. I’d love to work with you so I can use my talents to help the company do an even better job.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">你还可以把自己的优势和公司业务联系起来，比如：“我一直喜欢与人打交道，我也知道贵公司专注于帮助别人。我很希望加入你们，用我的能力帮助公司把工作做得更好。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You may be asked, “Why should we hire you?” This is your chance to explain what makes you a strong employee—being on time, efficient, organized, good at solving problems, and able to finish tasks by the deadline.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">面试官可能会问：“我们为什么要录用你？”这时你要说明自己作为员工的优势，比如守时、高效、有条理、善于解决问题、能按时完成任务等。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>A natural way to answer is to give specific strengths, such as: “I’m always on time. I’m efficient and organized. I enjoy learning new things, I like solving problems, and I like getting things done on time.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">比较自然的回答方式是给出具体优点，例如：“我一直很守时。我做事高效、有条理。我喜欢学习新事物，也喜欢解决问题，并且会按时把事情完成。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Another common question is, “Where do you see yourself in five years?” Be honest about your long-term plans—if you want to stay in a similar role, say so; if you want to grow into leadership, explain that you hope to earn promotions over time.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">另一个常见问题是：“五年后你希望自己在哪里/成为什么样？”要诚实说明长期规划：如果你希望五年后仍在类似岗位并把工作做得更好，就直说；如果你希望往管理层发展，也可以说明你想通过晋升逐步成长。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>If you want to “work your way up,” it means you want promotions—for example, starting in an entry role and eventually moving into roles like foreman or manager, and possibly into company leadership.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">如果你想“逐步往上升”，意思就是希望获得晋升，例如从基层岗位开始，逐渐做到组长/主管、经理，甚至进入公司的管理层或领导层。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You might be asked, “What motivates you?” Motivation is what makes you work hard and get things done, and people are motivated in different ways—some are self-motivated, some work best with a clear schedule and agenda, and some are motivated by a strong manager who gives clear direction.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">你可能会被问到：“什么能激励你？”所谓动机，就是促使你努力工作、把事情做成的因素。不同的人被不同的方式激励：有人自我驱动；有人需要清晰的日程与计划；也有人在明确指令、管理到位的领导带领下表现最好。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You might say, “I’m self-motivated. I usually enjoy my work, and I work hard whether someone is watching me or not.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">你可以说：“我比较自我驱动。我通常很享受工作，不管有没有人监督，我都会认真把事情做好。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You might be asked, “Describe your last job.” They want a brief, clear description of what you did—your role, responsibilities, schedule, and what you learned or enjoyed.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">你可能会被问到：“描述一下你上一份工作。”他们通常想听到一个简短清晰的概述：你的岗位是什么、主要做什么、工作节奏如何，以及你喜欢或收获了什么。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>For example: “I was a fifth-grade teacher at an elementary school. I worked full-time, and I taught all subjects,” or “I was a waiter at a restaurant. I worked there for seven years, often on weekend shifts, and I enjoyed helping people.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">比如：“我在一所小学当五年级老师。我是全职，负责教授各科课程。”或者：“我在一家餐厅当服务员。我在那里做了七年，经常上周末班，我很享受为顾客提供帮助的过程。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Another question is, “What led you to this career?” Explain what brought you into the field—your interests, education, and how you got involved.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">另一个问题是：“是什么让你选择了这个职业方向？”你可以解释自己为何进入这个领域：兴趣点、学习背景，以及你是如何开始从事这份工作的。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>For example, a teacher might say, “I’ve always loved working with kids, and teaching comes naturally to me.” A chef might say, “I’ve always loved food and preparing meals for other people.” Someone in programming might say, “I’ve always loved numbers, logic, and technology, so programming was a natural fit.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">例如，老师可以说：“我一直喜欢和孩子相处，教书对我来说很自然。”厨师可以说：“我一直很喜欢食物，也喜欢为别人准备餐点。”做编程的人可以说：“我一直喜欢数字、逻辑和科技，所以编程对我来说很适合。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You may be asked, “What is your greatest strength?” or “What are some of your strengths?” Be honest and specific—maybe you’re a strong problem solver, you’re great at planning, or you’re good with people and enjoy encouraging and motivating others.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">你可能会被问到：“你最大的优势是什么？”或“你有哪些优势？”要诚实且具体：比如你善于解决问题、擅长做规划，或者你很会与人沟通，喜欢鼓励和激励他人。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>If working with others is a strength, you can say: “One of my strengths is that I work well with others. I enjoy working on teams and collaborating with people.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">如果你的优势是团队协作，你可以说：“我的一个优势是我很擅长与人合作。我喜欢在团队里工作，与同事协同完成任务。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>This often leads to: “What is your greatest weakness?” or “What are some of your weaknesses?” Be honest about a real weakness, especially in how you work, and consider pairing it with how you manage it.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">这通常会引出：“你最大的弱点是什么？”或“你有哪些弱点？”你需要诚实说明一个真实的弱点，尤其是工作方式上的，并且最好补充你如何应对与改进。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>For example: “I work best on my own,” or “I get bored with repetitive tasks, so I prefer having some variety in my day.” You can mention the weakness while also showing self-awareness and a constructive approach.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">例如：“我更擅长独立完成任务。”或者：“我对重复性工作比较容易感到无聊，所以我更喜欢每天有一些变化。”你可以在说明不足的同时，展现自我认知和积极改进的态度。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You might be asked, “What is your biggest professional achievement?” Be ready to talk about something that went very well in a previous role—like a major problem you solved or a big project you finished successfully.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">你可能会被问到：“你最大的职业成就是什么？”你要准备讲一件以前做得非常成功的事情，比如你解决过的一个重大问题，或你顺利完成过的一项大型项目。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You might also hear: “Have you ever had a big problem at work, and how did you overcome it?” They want to see your problem-solving skills and how you think on your feet when things don’t go well.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">你也可能会听到：“你在工作中遇到过很大的问题吗？你是怎么解决的？”他们想看你的解决问题能力，以及当情况不顺时你能否快速思考、及时应对。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Another question is, “What do you like to do outside of work?” Be prepared to talk about your hobbies—reading, hiking, or anything you enjoy—because employers often ask this even though it’s not directly about the job.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">另一个问题是：“你工作之外喜欢做什么？”你要准备谈谈自己的爱好，比如阅读、徒步，或任何你喜欢的事情。尽管这不直接和工作相关，但雇主确实经常会问。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>You may be asked, “Do you work well with others?” If the role involves teamwork, be honest—if you enjoy teamwork, say so and give examples; if you prefer working independently, you can say you prefer solo work but can collaborate when needed.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">你可能会被问到：“你擅长与他人合作吗？”如果岗位需要团队协作，要诚实回答：如果你喜欢团队合作，就说明并给出例子；如果你更偏好独立工作，也可以说你更喜欢独立完成，但需要时也能与他人协作。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Finally, you’ll likely hear, “Do you have any questions for us?” Use this moment to ask practical and thoughtful questions—like expected hours per week or what a typical day looks like—and show you’ve learned something about the company.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">最后，你很可能会被问到：“你有什么问题想问我们吗？”这是提问的好机会：可以问每周工时、典型工作日是怎样的等务实问题，也可以提出基于你对公司了解的、更有针对性的问题，体现你做过功课。</div>
