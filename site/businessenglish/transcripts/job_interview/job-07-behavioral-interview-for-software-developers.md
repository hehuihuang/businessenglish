<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Many engineers do well in technical interviews, but get rejected because they struggle with the behavioral part. Behavioral interviews evaluate communication skills and how you handle real situations at work.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">很多工程师技术面试表现很好，但会因为行为面试不过而被拒。行为面试主要评估沟通能力，以及你在工作中如何处理真实情境。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>To prepare, brainstorm 8 to 10 stories from your career that you can reuse across different questions (for example: conflict, persuasion, making a technical recommendation, or learning from a mistake).</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">准备时可以先从自己的经历里整理出 8 到 10 个可复用的故事（例如：冲突处理、说服他人、提出技术建议、从错误中学习等），用来回答不同类型的问题。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>The strongest answers usually sound like a story: explain the situation, what you did, and the result. Keep it focused, and avoid adding fluff that doesn’t help answer the question.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">最好的回答通常像讲故事：交代情境，然后说明你做了什么，最后给出结果。内容要聚焦，不要加与问题无关的“填充内容”。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>“Tell me about yourself” is often the first question. A good approach is to briefly cover who you are, your core strengths, and a few relevant experiences that match the role.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">“请自我介绍”通常是第一题。比较好的结构是：简要说明你是谁、你的核心优势，以及几段与岗位强相关的经历。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Example answer: “Hi, I’m Sam. I’m a software engineer who enjoys solving complex problems. I’ve worked as a full-stack web developer across different layers of the stack, and I focus on building user-friendly, efficient web applications. I became interested in web app development during an internship at AWS, where I owned a solo project end-to-end—from design decisions to implementation—and gained exposure to AWS cloud tools. Since then, I’ve built dozens of applications over about three years. In my current role, I help modernize our infrastructure by building new microservices and updating existing services. My background includes .NET for APIs and server-side development, SQL across MySQL, Oracle, and SQL Server, and JavaScript frameworks like Vue.js and Angular. I believe this mix of experience and passion would make me a strong fit for your team.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">示例回答：“你好，我叫 Sam。我是一名软件工程师，喜欢解决复杂问题。我做过全栈 Web 开发，能覆盖技术栈的不同层面，并专注于构建用户友好且高效的 Web 应用。我对 Web 应用开发的兴趣始于在 AWS 的实习，当时我负责一个独立项目，从设计决策到落地实现全程主导，也接触了不少 AWS 云计算工具。此后大约三年里，我参与并交付了数十个应用。在目前的工作中，我主要做基础设施现代化，包括新建微服务以及升级现有服务。我熟悉用 .NET 开发 API 和服务端代码，掌握 MySQL、Oracle、SQL Server 等数据库的 SQL，也有 Vue.js、Angular 等前端框架经验。我相信这些经验与热情能让我成为团队的有力成员。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Keep your “tell me about yourself” answer short and relevant—about 1.5 to 2 minutes is a good target. Don’t drift into personal topics that don’t support your candidacy.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">自我介绍要短、要相关，控制在大约 1 分半到 2 分钟比较合适。不要跑题聊与岗位无关的私事。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Persuasion example: “On a previous team, we supported 20 to 30 web apps and used many different front-end frameworks (Angular, React, Vue). That lack of consistency slowed development when engineers had to learn a new framework before implementing features. I proposed standardizing on Vue.js because it was lightweight and easier to adopt. I built a small demo, presented pros and cons to my manager and team, and got buy-in. After that, we chose Vue.js for new development.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">说服案例：“在上一家公司，我们要维护 20 到 30 个 Web 应用，但前端框架非常不统一（Angular、React、Vue 都有）。当工程师被分配到不熟悉的框架时，需要先学习才能开发功能，导致周期变长。我建议把新项目统一到 Vue.js，因为它轻量、上手快，也更容易融入既有系统。我做了一个小 Demo，整理并展示了优缺点，约了团队和经理开会说明，最终获得认可，后来新开发基本都用 Vue.js。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Failure example: “I once worked directly on the master branch and kept pushing unfinished code. That’s risky because master should stay deployable and aligned with production. When an urgent hotfix was needed, we couldn’t deploy safely. I learned to create a feature branch off master, work and deploy from that branch, and merge back only when the feature is complete.”</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">失败案例：“我曾经直接在 master 分支上开发并持续推送未完成的代码。这很危险，因为 master 应该始终可部署并与生产环境保持一致。后来遇到紧急 Bug 修复时，我们无法安全发布。这个经历让我养成习惯：从 master 拉出功能分支，在分支上开发和验证，功能完成后再合并回 master。”</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Don’t lose your interviewer by going too deep into technical details they may not know. Adjust your explanation to the audience, and prioritize clarity over jargon.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">不要陷入过深的技术细节，把面试官听“迷路”。根据对方背景调整表达，优先保证清晰易懂，而不是堆术语。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>Key takeaways: Keep answers around 1 to 3 minutes, structure them as a story (situation → action → result), and simplify technical details when needed.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">核心要点：回答控制在 1 到 3 分钟；用“情境 → 行动 → 结果”的故事结构；必要时简化技术细节，确保面试官能理解。</div>
