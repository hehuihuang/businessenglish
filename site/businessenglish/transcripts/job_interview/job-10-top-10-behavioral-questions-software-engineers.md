<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>These are common behavioral questions in software engineering interviews. Keep your answers clear and concise, and go deeper only when the interviewer asks follow-up questions.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">这些是软件工程面试里很常见的行为题。回答要清晰、简洁，细节留给面试官追问时再展开。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>1. Tell me about yourself: Keep it short, simple, and memorable. Focus on a few points you want the interviewer to remember.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">1. 请自我介绍：要短、要清楚、要好记。抓住 2 到 3 个你希望对方记住的重点来讲。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>2. Tell me about a project you worked on: Explain the challenges, successes, and impact of your work on the team.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">2. 讲一个你做过的项目：说明项目难点、成果，以及你对团队/业务产生的影响。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>3. Disagreement questions: Describe a time you disagreed with a manager or cross-functional partner, and show how you listened, collaborated, compromised, and reached a good outcome.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">3. 分歧/冲突类问题：讲你与经理或跨团队伙伴发生分歧的经历，重点展示你如何倾听、协作、妥协，并最终达成双赢的结果。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>4. Technical fluency: Tell a story about explaining a technical concept to a non-technical person. Translate jargon into plain language.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">4. 技术表达能力：讲一次你把技术概念解释给非技术同事的经历，把术语转成对方听得懂的“人话”。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>5. Handling constraints: Share how you handled deadlines or tradeoffs (for example, refactor vs. ship). Explain your options, reasoning, and the impact.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">5. 约束与取舍：说明你如何应对紧迫截止期或权衡取舍（例如“重构 vs 先上线”），讲清楚你的备选方案、选择理由和最终影响。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>6. Weakness you’re working on: Be honest and self-aware, and show that you’re coachable and actively improving.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">6. 你正在改善的弱点：坦诚、具备自我认知，并体现你愿意被指导、在持续改进。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>7. Career goals: Be upfront about how you want to grow (industry, projects, front-end vs. back-end, manager vs. IC) and align it with the company’s direction.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">7. 职业目标：直说你想如何成长（行业方向、项目类型、前端/后端、走管理还是专家路线），并尽量与公司的发展方向对齐。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>8. Why do you want to work for this company? Do your research, understand the company’s goals, and connect them to your interests and strengths.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">8. 为什么想来这家公司？提前做功课，理解公司的目标与方向，并说明它如何与你的兴趣和优势匹配。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>9. Why do you want to leave your last company? Stay respectful, explain what didn’t work, and focus on why the new role is a better fit. If laid off, be honest and calm about the situation.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">9. 为什么离开上一家公司？不要贬低前公司，客观说明不匹配之处，并强调新机会更适合你。如果是裁员，也要坦诚、冷静说明情况。</div>
---

<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;"><strong>10. What are you looking for in your next role? Share what you’re excited about (mentorship, learning, technical growth) and what kind of team relationship you want.</strong></div>
<div style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.7;">10. 你在下一份工作中期待什么？说明你最看重的点（导师机制、学习机会、技术成长等），也可以表达你希望的团队协作与管理方式。</div>
